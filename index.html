Today

OUTSIDE THE NORMS

Imagine feeling like a walking joke every time you walk down the street. Imagine questioning your own identity time and time again because it's common to think one's gender is valid as long as it stands on one of the two extremes of the binary. Imagine being punished for no longer being able to fit in the box they say you belong in and for refusing to do so because living your life by other people's standards is not your duty. Imagine understanding that when people make too much emphasis on the type of chromosomes you have they're trying to tell you that you should act accordingly and stop pretending. Imagine being told you're making your life more difficult and that you should tone it down a little to make the ones around you more comfortable, otherwise it'll be your fault if anyone reacts disrespectfully. Imagine denying yourself to be who you are simply because you don't have the energy or strength to face those who don't condone your existence and those who have the audacity to act against it.

I guess policing each other's gender identity is more fun than respecting and acknowledging the people you COEXIST with and minding your own business. Either way, SHUT THE FUCK UP with your hate speeches and let ALL OF US be.
